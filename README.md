# Maketsu Studio

Maketsu Studio is a logo design and branding studio focused on creating distinctive, minimal, and timeless visual identities.

We help brands stand out through strategic design that is not only visually strong but also meaningful and scalable across platforms.

---

## 🎯 What We Do

- Logo Design
- Brand Identity Systems
- Visual Direction & Style Guides
- Typography & Color Systems
- Brand Refresh & Rebranding

---

## 🧠 Our Approach

At Maketsu Studio, design is not just about aesthetics.

We focus on:

- Clarity over complexity  
- Strong visual recall  
- Versatility across digital and print  
- Long-term brand consistency  

Each logo is designed to work across multiple use cases — from social media to packaging to websites.

---

## 🔍 Process

1. Discovery — Understanding the brand, audience, and vision  
2. Research — Competitor and industry analysis  
3. Concept Development — Multiple directions and explorations  
4. Refinement — Iteration based on feedback  
5. Final Delivery — Scalable assets and brand-ready files
